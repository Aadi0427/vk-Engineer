About Us

Who We Are

V.K. Engineers Established in 1989 in Faridabad (Haryana), V.K. Engineers is a trusted service provider offering Aluminium, MS, SS, Copper, and Metal Cutting Job Work. Our services are widely appreciated in engineering and automobile industries for their quality, speed, flexibility, and timely execution. We use advanced machinery and have a skilled team of engineers, technicians, and quality experts who ensure that every job is completed with high precision and as per client specifications. Our client-centric approach, easy payment options, and affordable pricing have helped us earn strong goodwill in the market. We credit our growth and success to the continuous guidance of our mentors, Mr. Madan Lal and Mr. Rajat Thakur.

From prototype development to bulk production, we ensure accuracy, durability and consistency in every project — making us the preferred partner for many industrial clients.
